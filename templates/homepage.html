About Mr Reed.

Mr Reed has over 25 years experience in the jewellery business and is based in Brighton. Seven of those years were spent as an apprenticeship to hone the craft. He also owns a fully equipped workshop for repairs and custom orders.

He has experience with working with gold, silver and platinum to order or his own style of design. He also allows customers to have their own custom designs created with private commissions. He will also buy any unwanted jewellery and sell jewellery he has designed.

If you are a Harry Potter fan, he has done work for the Great Frog which designed and created most of the jewellery in Harry Potter.
